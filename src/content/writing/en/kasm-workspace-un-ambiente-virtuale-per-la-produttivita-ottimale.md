## Kasm Workspace: An Introduction
**Kasm Workspace:** Kasm Workspace is an application and desktop virtualization platform designed to provide a complete and secure user experience. This solution allows access to applications and desktops from any device without worrying about compatibility or local installations.

## Why Kasm Workspace?

2.  **Advanced Security:** With advanced security features such as end-to-end encryption and access control, Kasm Workspace protects data and ensures compliance with regulations.
3.  **Isolated Environment:** Kasm Workspace offers a isolated working environment, avoiding conflicts or interference between applications, improving stability and security.
