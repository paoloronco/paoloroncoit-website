**Understanding AirCrack-ng:**  
AirCrack-ng is a set of open-source tools used for testing and evaluating the security of Wi-Fi networks. Among its main features are packet capture, network traffic analysis, and execution of various types of attacks, such as deauthentication and cracking of security keys. This suite of tools is widely used by professionals in information security and enthusiasts who wish to strengthen the security of wireless networks.

**Implementing AirCrack-ng on Raspberry Pi:**
