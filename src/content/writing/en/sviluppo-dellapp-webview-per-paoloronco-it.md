_Software development with Android Studio and Java_  
The realization of this app was possible thanks to the use of Android Studio, the official integrated development environment (IDE) for Android. This tool offers a wide range of features to simplify the process of developing Android apps and ensure an excellent user experience.

The programming language chosen for creating the app is Java, known for its stability and versatility in Android app development. The choice of Java ensures a robust and performant application.

_Key Features of the App_  
My personal portfolio app offers a series of features designed to highlight my work and skills:

1.  Curriculum Display: Users can easily access my curriculum, where they can explore my certifications, studies, and professional skills.
2.  Project Gallery: The app presents a visual gallery of my most significant projects, allowing users to examine my work in detail.
3.  Direct Contact Information: Users can find direct contact information to communicate with me quickly and simply.
4.  Custom Notifications: The app can send notifications about updates to my portfolio, ensuring that users are always informed about my latest achievements.
