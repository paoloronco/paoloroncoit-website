**Introduction**

In the world of information security, Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) is a key element for protecting digital resources. In this article, I will share my experience in implementing Wazuh SIEM on Linode, a cloud hosting service provided by Akamai. This tool has been fundamental in increasing both security and visibility of my online infrastructure.

**Choosing Linode**

The decision to use Linode as the platform for hosting the Wazuh SIEM was influenced by several factors. Linode's reputation for stability and reliability, combined with its scalability, made this platform an excellent choice for hosting a critical application like a SIEM.

**Implementing Wazuh SIEM**

Wazuh SIEM is an open-source solution that offers threat detection and log analysis. While its configuration and customization can be complex, the control it provides over system security is unparalleled.

I initially installed the Wazuh server on Linode and configured sensors to collect logs from all my servers. This centralized log collection allowed for effective and real-time analysis of suspicious activities across the entire infrastructure.

**Benefits of Wazuh SIEM**

The implementation of Wazuh SIEM has brought significant benefits in terms of security:

1.  **Threat Detection:** The system is capable of detecting and notifying suspicious activities in real time, allowing for rapid response to threats.
2.  **Global Visibility:** Centralized log collection provides a global view of the infrastructure, facilitating management and monitoring.
3.  **Scalability:** Linode offers the ability to increase resources as needed, enabling it to handle increasing volumes of logs.

**An Important Message**

Information security is a fundamental priority, and threat detection is an essential part of this equation. However, it's important to remember that log collection and data analysis come with significant responsibilities. Privacy and legal compliance must be respected throughout all phases of this process.

**Conclusions**

The implementation of Wazuh SIEM on Linode has been a crucial step in improving the security of my online infrastructure. The combination of a robust hosting platform and a powerful threat detection tool has allowed for unprecedented visibility and control. Information security is an ever-evolving area, and investing in tools and services like Wazuh SIEM on a reliable platform such as Linode represents an important step towards effective defense against digital threats.
