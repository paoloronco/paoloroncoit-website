In the era where IT certifications can accelerate a career, I felt the need to create **? [CertJourney.tech](https://certjourney.tech)**, a platform that serves as **an organized and up-to-date database of tech certifications** integrating **real reviews and an active community** where you can discuss and grow.

![](/posts/certjourney-tech-scoprire-confrontare-e-discutere-certificazioni-tech/image-1-1024x491.png)

### ? Project Objective

The objective was to **simplify the journey for those who wish to grow in the tech sector**, addressing these common issues:

-   Time wasted searching for certifications scattered across different websites.
-   Difficulty understanding which certifications have real career and skill value.
-   Lack of a community where you can compare experiences, share career advice, study tips, and reliable resources.

**CertJourney** helps those studying and working in tech to **discover, compare, and monitor certifications systematically**, promoting informed choices.

* * *

### ?️ Technologies and Stack Used

During development, I decided to integrate:

-   **Lovable.dev** as a low-code/AI-assisted framework to speed up prototyping and UI.
-   **Firebase Authentication** for login/signup via:
    -   Email/Password
    -   OAuth Google, GitHub, LinkedIn
-   **Firestore Database** to manage certification collections, reviews, and community posts securely, scalably, and with fine-grained permission rules.
-   **Node.js + CSV Script** for bulk imports of certifications, optimizing times and avoiding manual entries.
-   **Custom domain management and transactional email handling** (password reset, account confirmation) with the `@certjourney.tech` domain.
