I am excited to welcome you to my new website, which will serve as a portfolio of my projects and experiences with various software, personal projects, operating systems, and much more. This site has been created using WordPress, hosted in a Proxmox container, and auto-hosted via CloudFlare. It is my way of sharing my passion for technology and my work in the field of computing with you.

_Why I Created this Site_  
I decided to create this online space to share my professional journey and experience with you. Over the years, I have accumulated knowledge and expertise in various fields, from programming languages to network solutions, operating systems to software applications. This site will allow me to document and share my discoveries and projects with anyone who is interested.

_Technologies Used_  
As I mentioned earlier, this site has been created using WordPress, one of the most popular platforms for website creation. Its flexibility and ease of use make it an excellent choice for all types of websites. Additionally, the site is hosted in a Proxmox container, a virtualization solution that allows me to efficiently manage server resources. Thanks to CloudFlare, I can ensure greater security and speed for the site, improving the browsing experience for visitors.

_What to Expect in the Future_  
Over time, I intend to share articles, tutorials, and projects on various technology-related topics. Free resources will be available to help anyone who wants to learn something new or deepen their knowledge. I hope this site can become a valuable resource for those interested in computing and technology.

Thank you for visiting my site and showing interest. Stay tuned for future updates and do not hesitate to contact me if you have questions or suggestions.
